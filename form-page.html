How To Play!

Player to go first will randomly be selected.
Player hits roll and scores as many points as the total shown...providing they donâ€™t roll a 1.
The player may continue rolling and accumulating points (but risk rolling a 1) or end his turn.
If the player rolls a 1 his turn is over, he loses all points he accumulated that turn, and automatically passes turn to the next player.
This play goes on until a winner is determined.
The first player to accumulate 100 or more points wins the game.
